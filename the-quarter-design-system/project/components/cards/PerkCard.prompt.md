Partner perk in the member rewards catalogue. Shows redeem / redeemed states.

```jsx
<PerkCard partner="The Pound Bar" category="Food & drink"
  perk="20% off brunch, Monday to Friday" expires="Ends 30 Jun" onRedeem={redeem} />
<PerkCard partner="Curzon Canterbury" category="Culture"
  perk="2-for-1 cinema tickets midweek" redeemed />
```
