Showcase card for a space (Main Space, Flexi Rooms, the Café). Photo on top, name, blurb, icon+label facts, Explore link. Hover lifts the card.

```jsx
<SpaceCard
  name="The Quarter Café"
  blurb="The cathedral view, the natural light and the breakfast. An open social space — not bookable, just ours."
  imageCaption="Café with cathedral view, plants, morning light"
  tag="Open social space"
  meta={[{icon:'coffee',label:'Lavazza & breakfast'},{icon:'leaf',label:'Plantspiration'}]}
/>
```
